Introduction

Discover the different aspects and specialities, the next show and history of Cirque du soleil

At the dawn of the 1980s, a troupe of artists founded by Gilles Ste-Croix demonstrated their talents in the streets of Baie-Saint-Paul, a charming village nestled on the shores of the St. Lawrence River near Quebec City. Known to the public as The Stiltwalkers of Baie-Saint-Paul, this troupe formed the embryo of what was to become the Cirque du Soleil. This troupe, which gave an eclectic show featuring jugglers, dancers, fire-eaters and musicians, charmed the region's audience with its act. Little did these early fans and performers know that something magical, exciting and revolutionary would emerge from these early days that would have worldwide repercussions.
